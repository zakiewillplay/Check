01

Introduction

Thank you for visiting the Amity University Online website. We are committed to protecting your privacy. As a general rule, we do not collect any personal information about you unless you voluntarily provide it.

We do not sell, share, or transfer your personal information to third parties.

02

Non-Personal Information We Collect

If you browse our website without submitting any personal details, our system may automatically collect certain general information about your visit. During your visit, we may record:

  • Your internet domain (e.g., xyz.com, xyz.net, or yourcollege.edu)
  • The type and version of your web browser
  • Your operating system (e.g., Windows, macOS, or Unix)
  • The date and time of your visit, along with the pages you access
  • The website you visited prior to arriving at our site (if applicable)

This information is used solely for statistical and analytical purposes to improve the usability and performance of our website. It does not identify you personally.

03

OTP Verification for Payments

For secure transactions, we use One-Time Password (OTP) authentication. When making a payment, you may receive an OTP via SMS on your registered mobile number to verify and complete the transaction.

04

Cookies

We use cookies on certain pages to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help maintain your session while navigating the website. These cookies do not collect personal information.

You may choose to accept or decline cookies through your browser settings. For added privacy, we recommend closing your browser after completing your session.

05

Information from Emails

If you contact us via email, we may use the information you provide — including your email address — to respond to your inquiry.

Please note that email communication may not always be secure, so avoid sharing sensitive information unnecessarily.

06

Consent & Authorization

By registering with Amity Online, you authorize us and our associates to contact you via email, SMS, WhatsApp, or phone calls between 9:00 a.m. and 9:00 p.m. regarding updates, services, and course-related information.

This consent applies even if your number is registered under DND / NDNC / NCPR services and remains valid until your account is deactivated.

07

Information Gathered Through Interactive Forms

On certain pages of our website, we provide interactive forms that allow you to voluntarily share personal details such as your name, email address, or organization. This typically happens when you:

  • Register for counseling sessions, tests, quizzes, seminars, workshops, or training programs offered by Amity Online
  • Download admission forms or related documents

In such cases, the information you provide is used solely for its intended purpose and is not shared with any third party .

08

Links to External Sites and Partners

This policy outlines the privacy practices for the Amity Online website. However, our website may include links to third-party sites. Once you leave the Amity Online website, you will be accessing platforms that are not under our control .

External websites may use their own cookies, collect data, or request personal information. The privacy practices described here do not apply to such third-party sites. We strongly recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any external website you visit, especially before sharing personal information.

09

User Authorization

By using Amity University Online services and/or registering on the platform, you consent to be contacted by Amity University Online, including its representatives, affiliates, and business partners, via email, phone calls, or SMS . These communications may relate to the programs or courses you have selected, as well as sharing relevant information about them.

You acknowledge and agree that such communication may occur even if your contact details are registered under DND, DNC, or NCPR services. This consent will remain valid until your account is deactivated, either by you or by us.

10

Internship / Job Opportunities

Eligibility for internships or job opportunities will be determined based on merit and evaluated on a case-by-case basis .
